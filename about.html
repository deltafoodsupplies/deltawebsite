About Delta Food Supplies

Serving Texas food businesses since 2019.

Delta Food Supplies supports Indian and South Asian food demand across Texas with dependable wholesale products, responsive service, and practical distribution support.

Built for Indian grocery stores, restaurants, caterers, and foodservice teams.

Since 2019, Delta Food Supplies has worked with more than 300 customers across Dallas, Houston, Austin, and surrounding Texas communities. Our role is to make sourcing easier for businesses that need consistent Indian groceries, frozen products, dairy, snacks, sweets, and Nanak favorites.

We are headquartered at 11309 Indian Trail, Dallas, TX 75229, where eligible customers can coordinate in-person pickup. Businesses outside Dallas can contact our team about shipping and distribution options.
