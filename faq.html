Wholesale FAQ

Questions about Indian and South Asian food wholesale, pickup, truck delivery, and service areas.

Find answers about ordering from Delta Food Supplies and contact our sales team when you are ready for current product availability.

Where is Delta Food Supplies located?

Our headquarters is at 11309 Indian Trail, Dallas, TX 75229.

Do you offer in-person pickup?

Yes. Customers can coordinate in-person pickup from our Dallas HQ for eligible wholesale orders.

Do you offer pickup or delivery?

Yes. Eligible wholesale customers can pick up from one of our warehouses, and we also use in-house trucks to deliver orders on supported routes across Texas, New Mexico, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana, and surrounding markets.

What types of businesses do you serve?

We serve restaurants, grocery stores, caterers, foodservice teams, temples, and community organizations.

What products can I order?

We supply Indian and South Asian frozen foods, dairy, pantry goods, spices, snacks, sweets, restaurant supplies, and top-selling Nanak products.
